# Contributing

QRoPE is a patent-pending research repository. Contributions are welcome when they preserve reproducibility, evidence traceability, and the bounded publication posture.

## Ground rules

- Keep claims source-backed. New research claims should cite the file, packet, script, or experiment that supports them.
- Do not add secrets, private credentials, unpublished customer data, or private backend tokens.
- Do not broaden the claim posture beyond the evidence. Avoid claims of general quantum advantage, production-transformer superiority, or cross-backend robustness unless the repo contains direct evidence and patent review has cleared the wording.
- Preserve raw evidence. Prefer adding new packets over mutating historical evidence packets.
- Keep scripts deterministic where practical. Document random seeds, fixed packet identifiers, shot counts, backend metadata, and verifier settings.

## Licensing

By contributing, you agree that your contribution is provided under `AGPL-3.0-only` for this repository unless a separate written agreement states otherwise.

## Research-review expectations

Pull requests that affect publication, patent scope, or evidence interpretation should include:

- a short claim-boundary note;
- links to supporting evidence;
- a statement of whether the change affects the provisional-support audit;
- any known limitations or negative evidence.

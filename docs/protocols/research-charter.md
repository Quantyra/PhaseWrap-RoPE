# QRoPE Research Charter Protocol

## Purpose
Define operating boundaries, evidence standards, and decision gates for Quantum Rotational Positional Embeddings research.

## Scope
- Theory and mathematics of rotational positional encoding variants.
- Numerical and algorithmic analysis of stability, expressivity, and scaling behavior.
- Implementation planning for reproducible experiments.

## Non-goals
- Product claims without validated evidence.
- Publication-ready novelty claims before novelty review gate completion.
- Safety or compliance bypasses for schedule reasons.

## Required decision gates
1. Problem framing gate: clear hypothesis and measurable outcomes.
2. Evidence gate: all high-impact claims have source tags or explicit inference labels.
3. Reproducibility gate: experiments have enough detail to rerun.
4. Novelty gate: prior-art and overlap scan completed before formal claim hardening.
5. Closure gate: story outputs and risks updated before status is set complete.

## Claim labeling policy
Each non-trivial claim must be marked as one of:
- `Taken from source`
- `Inferred for repo`

## Escalation triggers
- Conflicting high-impact sources with no resolution path.
- Missing evidence for central assumptions.
- Material reproducibility gaps.


## Planning Rule
- Future research cycles must also follow docs/protocols/research-planning.md so new work begins from a missing question, decision leverage, and explicit stop conditions.

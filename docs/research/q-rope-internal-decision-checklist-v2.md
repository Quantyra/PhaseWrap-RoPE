# Q-RoPE Internal Decision Checklist v2

Date: 2026-03-14
Stories: S1212-S1213

## Review Questions
- [ ] Is the standing benchmark still the correct internal reference?
- [ ] Are the preserved positive transfer families sufficient for current internal confidence?
- [ ] Are the archived transfer boundaries being treated as real boundaries rather than ignored exceptions?
- [ ] Does the bridge portfolio support a selective positional-relevance interpretation without overclaiming?
- [ ] Are the archived bridge boundaries being treated as real evidence rather than near-misses?
- [ ] Are hardware and publication still correctly out of scope?
- [ ] Is there any missing positional question important enough to justify one new screened candidate later?

## Decision Options
### Preserve And Pause
Use if the current package is enough and no future candidate is worth screening soon.

### Continue At Low Intensity With No New Execution
Use if the package should remain active internally, but no current candidate deserves execution.

### Authorize One Materially Different Screened Candidate Review Later
Use only if a candidate clearly answers a missing positional question and passes the existing intake, structural, non-compressibility, and bounded-fairness screens.

## Do Not Approve
- hardware reopening
- publication push
- unscreened execution branch
- automatic transfer or bridge proliferation
- same-family symbolic escalation by inertia

# Q-RoPE Symbolic Insufficiency Future Family Admissibility v1

Date: 2026-03-11
Stories: S844

## Purpose
Define the minimum bar for any future symbolic challenger family before code can reopen.

## Admissibility Criteria
A future symbolic family is admissible only if all of the following are true:
- it is materially different from the current polynomial/atlas ladder rather than one more degree extension
- it freezes one exact basis at memo level before implementation approval
- it preserves auditability and boundedness
- it does not introduce latent path ids, exact microstate keys, hidden tuple lookups, or uncontrolled basis growth
- it states why the new family is conceptually stronger than the existing benchmarked families, not just algebraically larger

## Non-Admissible Continuations
- another same-pattern `plus` family without a new representational idea
- arbitrary higher-order polynomial growth over the same atlas lattice
- any family justified only by `one more degree may catch up`

## Preferred Future Directions
- conceptually different symbolic families
- theory-led characterization of the witness vs frozen symbolic basis
- transfer or externalization planning instead of more same-family challenger generation

# Q-RoPE Transfer Cascade Reconciliation Task Family Design v1

## Intent
Open one materially new transfer family after the archived `braid` and `staggered-binding` boundaries.

## Core Structure
- Three-stage relational process with an explicit late reconciliation step.
- Earlier stages may diverge, but the target depends on whether the final reconciliation compresses that divergence in a way not recoverable from coarse declared summaries alone.

## Why This Is Materially Different
- Not path-local.
- Not loop closure.
- Not fork-join.
- Not relay-binding.
- Not braid crossing.
- Not staggered binding.

## Candidate Task
- `synthetic_symbolic_insufficiency_cascade_reconciliation_response`

## Candidate Witness
- `V_future_relational_witness_symbolic_insufficiency_cascade_reconciliation`

## Candidate Bounded Control
- additive and bounded-quadratic regressor over declared source/diverge/reconcile summaries only.
